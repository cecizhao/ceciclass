Conflict Management Style Quiz

Instructions

Listed below are 15 statements. Assign each statement a numerical value based on the following scale:

5 = Always 4 = Very often 3 = Sometimes 2 = Not very often 1 = Rarely, if ever

Statements

1. I argue my case with peers, colleagues, and coworkers to demonstrate the merits of the position I take.

我与同伴、同事和工作伙伴争论我的观点，以展示我所持立场的优点。

2. I try to reach compromises through negotiation.

我尝试通过协商达成妥协。

3. I attempt to meet the expectation of others.

我努力满足他人的期望。

4. I seek to investigate issues with others in order to find solutions that are mutually acceptable.

我寻求与他人一起调查问题，以找到双方都能接受的解决方案。

5. I am firm in resolve when it comes to defending my side of the issue.

在捍卫我对问题的立场时，我的决心很坚定。

6. I try to avoid being singled out, keeping conflict with others to myself.

我尽量避免成为焦点，将与他人的冲突埋在心里。

7. I uphold my solutions to problems.

我坚持我对问题的解决方案。

8. I compromise in order to reach solutions.

我妥协以达成解决方案。

9. I trade important information with others so that problems can be solved together.

我与他人交换重要信息，以便共同解决问题。

10. I avoid discussing my differences with others.

我避免与他人讨论我的分歧。

11. I try to accommodate the wishes of my peers and colleagues.

我尽量满足同伴和同事的愿望。

12. I seek to bring everyone's concerns out into the open in order to resolve disputes in the best possible way.

我寻求将每个人的关切都公开，以便以最佳方式解决争端。

13. I put forward middle positions in effort to break deadlocks.

我提出折中立场，努力打破僵局。

14. I accept the recommendations of colleagues, peers, and coworkers.

我接受同事、同伴和工作伙伴的建议。

15. I avoid hard feelings by keeping my disagreements with others to myself.

我通过将与他人的分歧保留给自己来避免不快。

Your Conflict Management Style Results

Style Animal Symbol Score Ranking

Understanding Your Results

🦈 Competing/Forcing (Shark):

You focus on achieving your goals at the expense of others. You use power to win.

🦉 Collaborating (Owl):

You seek to find a solution that fully satisfies everyone's concerns. You work through differences to find a win-win solution.

🐢 Avoiding (Turtle):

You do not pursue your own concerns or those of others. You stay away from conflicts.

🧸 Accommodating (Teddy Bear):

You neglect your own concerns to satisfy the concerns of others. You yield to others' points of view.

🦊 Compromising (Fox):

You seek a middle ground. You give up some points to gain others, finding expedient, mutually acceptable solutions.
